MeshVertexListComputeMeshArrayCleanUpThreshold Method

Analyzes some meshes, and determines the intrinsic minimal tolerance, which is the minimal vertex-to-vertex and vertex-to-edge distance. Also, informs the caller if any double precision vertex would transform into another different, existing one when converted to single precision.
